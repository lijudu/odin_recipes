Onigiri

Simple recipe for simple nights. Eyeballing measurements encouraged.

Ingredients

  • Rice (Asian kind preferred)
  • Rice vinegar
  • Canned tuna or salmon
  • Mayo
  • Nori
  • Optional- toasted sesame seeds

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix rice 1 cup of rice and 1 tablespoon rice vinegar together.
  2. In a separate bowl, mix canned tuna (or salmon) with 2 tablespoons of mayo.
  3. Use your hands to form a ball with the rice, then make an indent in the middle using your finger.
  4. Add a spoonful of tuna/mayo mix into the indent, then bring together rice from the side to form a ball.
  5. Wrap ball with nori sheet.
