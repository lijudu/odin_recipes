Gyudon

Easy meat and egg dish!

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Thinly sliced beef shank
  • Egg
  • Sliced onion
  • Soy sauce
  • Mirin
  • Sugar

Steps

  1. In a hot pan, add onion and bit of oil.
  2. When onions are cooked, add in beef.
  3. Add in 1:1:1 ratio of sugar, soy sauce, mirin
  4. Add beaten egg, then cover pan until beef and egg cooked.
